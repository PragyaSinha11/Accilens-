About the Project

What is AcciLens?

📍

Spatial Analysis

Identifies high-frequency accident zones using geographic clustering and density mapping across India's road network.

⏱️

Temporal Patterns

Reveals peak accident windows — by hour, weekday, and season — enabling targeted enforcement and infrastructure upgrades.

🔍

Root Cause Factors

Correlates weather, road type, speed limits, and lighting with accident frequency and severity outcomes.

📊

Government Data

Built on 50,000+ official records sourced from India's Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH).

🎓

Academic Capstone

Final-year college capstone project showcasing data analytics, Power BI, and full-stack web development.

🛡️

Safety Advocacy

AcciLens insights support policymakers, traffic authorities, and planners in reducing road fatalities across India.
